Neona Creation was born from the fervent desire of its founder, Neelam Lamba, who is striving to create a larger canvas than what exists today, for all the beautiful and unparalleled creativity that the simple artisans of India have been quietly perfecting for centuries. Her collection comes straight from the heart.

Neona Creation is a colour riot and skilfully combines design and innovation. It is based on the belief that artisanal craft is an integral part of the Indian heritage, something that should never die out and must be preserved for time immemorial.

The company is further driven by the vision to promote women entrepreneurship. We hope to be able to raise the dignity of the myriad women artisans, who possess this innate talent, and the Indian handicraft industry in our own small way. It is our effort to offer an undeniably unique selection of traditional art and craft translated into a range of attractive contemporary products, while bringing it closer to people across the world. We experiment with ideas and designs to innovate and create relevant craft products that appeal to changing tastes and modern preferences.

Neona Creation is a perfect blend of unique design, ethereal craftsmanship, and sustainability.

The most remarkable feature of Neona Creation is that Mrs. Lamba is herself piloting the design and conceptualization of the art and craft goods. She hails from Rajasthan, where she was born and bred. She later got married and moved to a small town in Punjab. Currently, she resides in Maharashtra. Therefore, the art and creativity that she brings forth is a magnetic confluence of the impact that the culture and traditions of these three regions had on her. Being the sole captain of the ship, she wants to lead by example, empower and propel other women artisans on the path to entrepreneurship. Through her work she seeks to communicate the message that housewives are the backbone of the society, who along with balancing house chores can also harness their inner talents if given the right platform and the much-needed push. Mrs Neelam Lamba hails from a generation where women are ordained to prioritise their children and family over their own aspirations and dreams. So once her responsibilities of rearing and nurturing a loving family were fulfilled, Mrs. Lamba decided to take the plunge and explore her passion of creating beautiful art by reusing and upcycling household materials from our daily life.
